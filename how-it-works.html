How It Works

From discovery to embedded support in four steps

We do not drop software and leave. NexaDataFlow consultants stay with your lab until automated workflows — and billing, if included — run reliably.

1. Discovery

We shadow your team, interview stakeholders, and inventory every system touchpoint: LIMS, instruments, billing, spreadsheets, and manual workarounds. For billing clients, revenue cycle mapping starts here too.

2. Workflow mapping

We document the current state and design the target state — where data is entered once, validated, and routed automatically. You approve before we build.

3. Implement and onboard

We configure integrations, automate handoffs, and embed consultants to train staff and handle the first weeks of exceptions. Billing operations plug into the same workflow map from day one when bundled.

4. Monitor and optimize

We track error rates, turnaround times, and denial trends. Continuous improvement is part of the engagement — not a change-order.

For billing + automation clients

When you sign up for medical billing alongside workflow automation, we integrate billing ops into the same discovery and mapping process. That means clean claims on day one of go-live — not months of cleaning up inherited data debt.
