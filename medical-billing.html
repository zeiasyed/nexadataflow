Medical Billing

Lab billing that gets better when workflows are unified

Most billing vendors receive dirty data and charge you to fix it. NexaDataFlow handles billing and data automation together — so claims go out cleaner, denials drop, and savings flow back to your lab.

Full-service lab billing

Our billing team knows diagnostic lab codes, payer quirks, and the operational reality of high-volume testing. Services include:

  • Claims creation and submission
  • Denial management and appeals
  • Payer follow-up and A/R monitoring
  • Coding support and charge capture review
  • Reporting you can actually use

The integrated advantage

When NexaDataFlow also owns your data workflows, billing staff are not re-keying results from PDFs or reconciling LIMS exports. Data enters once upstream — billing receives clean, complete claims downstream.

Industry data shows duplicate submissions remain a top denial category for labs. Unified workflows attack that problem at the source.

The savings flywheel

Bundling billing with workflow automation creates compounding efficiency — and we pass a meaningful share of that back to partner labs.

Unified data pipelines

Orders, results, and demographics flow automatically from LIMS to billing — no duplicate entry, fewer errors.

Lower rework cost

With fewer denials tied to bad data, your team spends less time on appeals and manual corrections. Industry benchmarks put denied-claim admin costs at $57+ per claim and rework at $25–$181 per denial.

Operational efficiency

One partner, one workflow map, one accountability chain. Less vendor coordination, faster issue resolution.

Savings passed to your lab

As our cost to serve decreases, we share that benefit — lower effective billing rates compared to standalone RCM vendors who inherit your data quality problems.
