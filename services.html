Services

Automation, consultants, and billing — built for clinical labs

NexaDataFlow is not a software vendor that disappears after go-live. We embed with your team and own outcomes across data workflows and, when you need it, revenue cycle.

Workflow automation

We map how data moves today — from order entry through LIMS, instruments, QC, and billing — then automate handoffs so information is captured once and propagated everywhere it needs to go.

  • LIMS and instrument integration
  • Order and result routing
  • Exception handling and alerts
B

Embedded lab consultants

Dedicated NexaDataFlow staff work inside your lab alongside your team. They learn your SOPs, attend your standups, and stay until workflows are stable — not until a ticket closes.

  • On-site or remote embedded support
  • Workflow documentation and training
  • Continuous improvement cadence
C

Duplicate-entry elimination

We target the specific re-keying loops that burn FTE hours: demographics copied into LIMS, results re-entered for billing, spreadsheets bridging systems that should talk to each other.

  • Single source of truth design
  • Validation at point of entry
  • Audit trails for compliance
